About Me

Hello, you can call me "Rob". I am originally from San Clemente, CA and now reside in the Tampa, FL area. I have migrated to the Tampa area after a six year enlistment in the United States Air Force. I worked as an FAA certified Air Traffic Controller during my enlistment which has given me many tools to work in dynamic and fast paced environments. After my six year enlistment, I decided to follow my passion of computers. I was accepted to the University of South Florida's Computer Science and Engineering degree program in 2018. Since then, I have been diligently learning and working towards my degree. In my free time, I enjoy working out and spending time with my fiance and dog. We love to travel and see new cities, cultures, and experience all types of food. Want to hear more? Feel free to contact me via email which is located in the page menu. I appreciate you taking the time to read my story, thank you.

Respectfully,
Rob
