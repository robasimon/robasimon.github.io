About Me

Hello, my name is Robinson Simon. I am originally from San Clemente, CA and now reside in the Tampa, FL area. I have migrated to the Tampa area after a six year enlistment in the United States Air Force. Projects

Flip Phone:



×

Developed and designed an Android application to sell smartphone devices. Utilizes Firebase database backend to store users, device photos, and smartphone listings. This application guides the user looking to sell a smartphone by providing on-screen instructions to obtain an optimal photo of the device. Once a listing is made, it will appear on the main app screen. Sorting and filtering options are available for those looking to purchase a device. Users can press on any listing on the main screen that will display more info about the device along with options to message listing owner. The idea was to improve an area of commerce that does not dedicate enough detail to smartphone sales.


Scheduling App:




Developed Android application to schedule work shifts. Firebase database stores registered user information. User can add employees and shifts to their account. They can also create, read, update, delete shifts and employees. Shifts are displayed on the main app screen and also in the app's built in calendar.
